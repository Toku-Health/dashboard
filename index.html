About This Dashboard

Data sources: This dashboard synthesises six data channels, refreshed daily via automated pipeline: Google Search Console (keyword rankings & organic traffic), Google Ads (paid keyword performance via Triple Whale), Triple Whale AI Visibility (brand mentions across ChatGPT & Gemini), Moz (domain authority, backlinks & trust signals (current DA: 17)), Google Analytics 4 (page-level sessions & engagement), and Technical SEO Audit (schema, LLM crawlability, page speed & content structure).

The growth model: Publish keyword-targeted content → rank in organic search → get cited by AI models as a trusted source → build domain authority through quality backlinks. Each channel reinforces the others: higher DA improves rankings, better rankings increase AI citations, and AI citations drive brand awareness that feeds organic traffic.

How to use: Start with the KPI summary cards for a quick health check. Jump to Actions & Recommendations for prioritized next steps, including the Domain Trust Action Plan for link-building strategy. Drill into Keywords, AI Visibility, or Content sections for deeper analysis.

Key objectives: (1) Rank top-5 for primary keywords (nattokinase, nattokinase supplement, nattokinase supplements), (2) close the attribution gap between AI source citations and brand mentions, (3) raise domain authority from current level to competitive baseline, and (4) optimise all content for both organic search and AI visibility using the 4-signal scoring model.
